🚀 Release Notes – June 2025 – Week 2

Explore the latest updates across personalization in workflow Replies.

⚙️ Location & Ticket-Based Personalizers in Workflow Replies

We’ve enhanced the Workflow Reply Action feature to support dynamic personalizers from both location profiles and ticket-related data. This allows your workflow replies to be more context-aware and personalized — saving time and improving communication quality.

🆕 What’s New

🔹 Location-Based Personalizers

Insert attributes from the associated location profile directly into your messages.

  • {{location.name}}
  • {{location.address}}
  • {{location.phone}}
  • {{location.business_hours}}
  • ✅ Custom location fields are also supported.

🔸 Ticket-Based Personalizers

You can now personalize replies using ticket-specific details as well.

  • {{ticket.id}}
  • {{ticket.customer_name}}
  • {{ticket.category}}
  • {{ticket.priority}}
  • {{ticket.created_date}}
  • {{ticket.status}}
  • {{ticket.assigned_agent}}
  • ✅ Custom ticket fields are also supported.

✅ Why It Matters

  • 📍 Location-specific contact info and hours
  • 🎫 Ticket context such as customer name, issue category, and priority
  • 🤝 More tailored communication based on where and why the customer reached out

This is especially valuable for businesses operating at scale across different branches and support teams.

💬 Example Usage

Hi {Full Name},  
Thanks for reaching out to {{location.name}}.  
We’ve received your ticket (#{{ticket.id}}) regarding {{ticket.category}}.  
You can visit us at {{location.address}} or call us at {{location.phone}}.  
We'll get back to you shortly.

Start using personalizers in your workflows today to deliver intelligent, dynamic responses to every customer — no matter where they are or what issue they’re facing.
